🏯 Puyuan — the Song-dynasty water town most Kiwi travellers have never heard of. And it's part of what makes authentic china experiences feel different on Best of China.

You'll find Suzhou, Hangzhou, Wuzhen in every "China water town" guide. Puyuan isn't in those. It sits 90 minutes from Hangzhou in the Jiangnan delta — a working town that happens to look like it's been preserved since the Song dynasty (10th-13th century).

What's there:
🌊 Waterways that still parallel cobbled lanes — boats deliver vegetables, not tourists
🏮 Paper lanterns lit at dusk over stone arch bridges
🏘️ Wooden gallery houses leaning toward each other across the canals
🍵 A tea house where the regulars are mostly elderly locals playing mahjong, not tour groups
🌸 Song-style architecture: tiled roofs, lattice windows, the geometry that influenced classical Japanese architecture 700 years later

Best of China spends Day 9 here. After lunch you walk the lanes; after dinner you watch the lanterns come on over the water. Then you sleep in a Puyuan Hotel — 5-star comfort, central location.

## 风险点 (Reviewer 关注)

1. **W2-11 是江南 gap 独家 USP 最强 post**: 5 竞品全没有 Hangzhou+Puyuan，CTS Best of China 独家。**这是 Wave 1 营销价值最高的一篇**。文案应该最大化"Kiwi 没听过 + CTS 独家"的稀缺感。
2. **GSC `/blog/china-water-towns-jiangnan-guide` 900 imp / 2 clicks**: CTS 已经有水乡博客在排名 (位置 #8.7)。W2-11 内链此页 = **boost 博客 CTR 双重 KPI**。这是 W2 第二个流量 boost 机会 (W2-10 是第一个 visa 博客)。
3. **"boats deliver vegetables, not tourists"**: 是 Puyuan 真实特色 (它不是 Wuzhen / Zhouzhuang 那种 over-tourism 水乡)。**安全 + 营销有力**。
4. **"influenced classical Japanese architecture 700 years later"**: 学术细节有争议。Conservative 版: "influenced East Asian architecture for centuries afterward"。但 Song 建筑确实通过 Tang/Song 文化输出影响日本是常识。**可保留**。
5. **"5-star Puyuan Hotel"**: tours.ts 真实 (Day 9 accommodation 5-star)。安全 + 给读者品质信号。
6. **3 张 carousel — IG 优先**: 这篇视觉冲击力强 (canal at dusk + gallery houses + tea house 三角度)，IG carousel 比 FB single image 更适合。
7. **"You don't get this on a 10-day Beijing-Xi'an-Shanghai itinerary"**: 暗示对手 (Wendy Wu / Trafalgar 10-day 团) 跳过 Jiangnan。Master_brief.competitive_notes_md 已经定位 CTS 反 mass-market。**安全 + 战略对齐**。
