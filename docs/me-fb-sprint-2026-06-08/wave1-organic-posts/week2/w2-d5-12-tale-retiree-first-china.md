## 风险点 (Reviewer 关注)

1. **"I don't want to die exhausted in a Shanghai hotel"**: 是通用 paraphrase 而非具体客户原话引用。**符合 R1 红线**。但 "die exhausted" 措辞**轻微暗黑幽默** — 60+ 受众可能觉得有点不吉利。**Conservative 版**: "I don't want to come home more tired than when I left" — 更安全。
2. **"travellers in their 60s and 70s"**: master_brief.primary_audience 35-70 涵盖。retiree 通用画像，安全。
3. **"two cities decide whether you understand 5,000 years of Chinese civilization"**: 大词 + civilizational depth claim — 给 retiree 受众 emotional / intellectual hook，跟"我等了 30 年"心理对齐。安全。
4. **"Day 4 free day in Xi'an / Day 8 buffer"**: Tale 团我未单独 read Day-by-day。**Conservative 版**: 删除具体 Day，改 "Free time included for personal pace, not 'next stop in 90 minutes'"。
5. **"Beijing 700 years / Xi'an 2,000+ years"**: 历史深度对比。Beijing 作为 capital ~700 年 (Yuan/Ming/Qing Dynasty 1271+)，Xi'an 作为 capital 2,000+ 年 (Western Zhou/Qin/Han/Tang)。**事实方向对**，给 retiree 受众正是想要的"civilizational layers"感。
6. **W2-12 vs W1-05 重复风险**: 两篇都是 Tale of Two Cities + Beijing+Xi'an 主题，间隔 W1-05 (Thu) → W2-12 (Fri) 一周。**不算重复**: W1-05 是 educational "decoded"，W2-12 是 social_proof "retiree 视角"。Audience 重叠但 angle 不同。
7. **CTA UTM `cts_tale_of_two_cities`**: 独立 Campaign UTM (跟 W1-05 一致)。子牙 ME marketing-plan 把 W1-05 + W2-12 + W4-22 都登记到 Tale Campaign Goal。
