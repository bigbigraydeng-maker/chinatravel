## 1. China Discovery — Shanghai & Surroundings

**Departure highlighted:** 14 October 2026 (as published; confirm year and availability at booking.)  
**Duration / from:** 10 days · NZD $2,999 per person (as published.)

### Section: Yangtze Delta in one journey

This Discovery itinerary is built around the **Yangtze Delta** — a corridor of gardens, lakeside towns, canal life, tea country, and finally **Shanghai** as your bookend metropolis. You begin from **Auckland** and fly to **Shanghai**, then move in a deliberate sequence through **Suzhou**, **Wuxi**, **Xinshi Ancient Town**, **Hangzhou**, and back to **Shanghai** for your final full days before departure.

The tour overview on our main product page lists the spine of the experience: **Suzhou’s Master of the Nets Garden** with **Panmen** and **Shantang Street**; **Wuxi**’s **Three Kingdoms City** (including the warship tour and horse battle show), **Purple Sand Museum**, **Nanchang Ancient Street**, **Li Garden** by the lake, and a **local market** day; **Hanfu** dress-up for photos and **afternoon tea** in **Xinshi**; **Hangzhou**’s **West Lake** boat tour, **Su Causeway**, **Leifeng Pagoda**, and **Hangzhou International Expo Center** (G20); **Six Harmonies Pagoda** and **Longjing tea tasting** at **Meijiawu**; then **Shanghai** — **the Bund**, the **City God Temple** quarter, the **World Cultural Heritage Art Exhibition Centre**, and **Nanjing Road**. That is the product — this page simply walks you through it in plain language.

### Section: Pacing, road legs, and where you stay

Daily detail lives in our published day-by-day itinerary; here is the **movement skeleton** exactly as timed on the tour page: **Shanghai to Suzhou** (about 1.5 hours), **Suzhou to Wuxi** (about 1 hour), **Wuxi to Xinshi Ancient Town** (about 1.5 hours), **Xinshi to Hangzhou**, then **Hangzhou to Shanghai** (about 2 hours). Land transport on tour is included as specified in the itinerary.

Accommodation is named at **4–5 star standard** on the product page for each segment — for example **Rosedale Chunshenhu Resort Hotel** or similar in Suzhou, **Holiday Inn Express Wuxi** or similar in Wuxi, **Holiday Inn** or similar in Xinshi, **New Century Hotel Hangzhou** or similar in Hangzhou, and **Holiday Inn Express Shanghai** or similar in Shanghai. Hotels remain “or similar” as published; final properties are confirmed for your departure.

### Section: Food, free time, and optional extras

**Meals:** Included meals are those **listed in the itinerary**; any meal not listed is not included. **Tips** are not included; the published suggestion on the tour page is **NZD $10 per person per day**.

**Optional activities (extra cost):** The itinerary highlights an **optional Shanghai acrobatics show** and **Maglev ride** — these are explicitly **not** included in the tour price. Personal expenses, transport and guide services during **free time**, and anything not specifically mentioned as included are likewise excluded.

### Section: Flights, guides, and what “all in” means here

**Included** on the published tour page: **international and domestic airfares**; **hotel accommodation** as specified; **English-speaking tour guide**; **entrance fees and meals as specified** in the itinerary; **land transfer**. **China visa fees** apply only if a **traditional visa** is required for your trip (as per the published exclusions). **Travel insurance** is **strongly recommended** and is **not** included.

### Section: October departure — 14 October

Your group departure date is published as **14 October** (with year **2026** where applicable on site — **always confirm at booking**). Use this section for a short **“how to hold a seat / what happens next”** call-to-action: enquiry form, phone **0800 CTS 888**, or email **info@ctstours.co.nz**, and our **Auckland** office address on **Queen Street** as published in the site footer.

### Section: How this differs from “A Tale of Two Cities”

**Shanghai & Surroundings** is a **single-region depth** journey: the **Delta + Shanghai**. It does **not** include **Beijing**, the **Great Wall**, or **Xi’an / Terracotta Warriors**. If those icons are your priority, compare our **A Tale of Two Cities** itinerary instead.

## 2. China Discovery — A Tale of Two Cities

**Departure highlighted:** 15 October 2026 (as published; confirm at booking.)  
**Duration / from:** 10 days · **From** NZD $3,480 (as published.)  
**“Two cities” on this product:** **Beijing** and **Xi’an** — connected by **high-speed train**. (Despite the URL slug, the published itinerary is **not** Beijing + Shanghai.)

### Section: Two chapters — imperial Beijing, ancient Xi’an

**A Tale of Two Cities** is structured as two full chapters in China’s north and central interior. **Beijing** delivers the imperial and modern icons named on the tour page: the **Temple of Heaven** in its garden setting, **Tiananmen Square**, the **Forbidden City**, the **Great Wall**, a photo stop at the **Bird’s Nest** and **Water Cube**, and **old Beijing** on a **pedi-cab Hutong tour** with a **family visit**.

You then travel by **high-speed train** to **Xi’an** for the second chapter: the **Terracotta Warriors**, **Xi’an’s ancient City Wall**, the **Tang Dynasty Big Wild Goose Pagoda**, the **Small Wild Goose Pagoda**, and **Huimin Street**. Every sight in this paragraph is taken directly from the published highlights and day-by-day structure.

### Section: The high-speed train leg

Day **6** on the published itinerary is **Beijing — Xi’an by high-speed train**. This is a core part of the product story: you experience China’s long-distance rail network as **included** land transport between the two cities — not as an optional add-on. Seat class and exact schedule follow operational confirmation for your departure.

### Section: Flights, hotels, and rhythm

International routing on the published page: **Auckland — Beijing** on Day 1 (evening departure, meals on board as specified), and return via **Beijing — Auckland** on the published final day pattern. **Hotels** are **4-star** throughout as published — **Beijing Wanda Moments** or similar in Beijing, **Mercure Xi’an Downtown** or similar in Xi’an.

### Section: Inclusions and exclusions (plain English)

**Included:** **international and domestic airfares**; **4-star hotel accommodation** throughout; **English-speaking tour guide**; **entrance fees** as specified; **meals** as specified; **land transfers**. **Not included:** **China visa** (only if a **traditional visa** is required), **travel insurance** (strongly recommended), **personal expenses**, **transport and guide during free time**, **meals not listed**, **tips** (suggested **NZD $10 per person per day**, as published on the tour page), and any item **not specifically mentioned as included**.

### Section: October departure — 15 October

Published departures for this tour include **13 August**, **15 October**, and **2 November** (year as per site — **confirm at booking**). For this campaign LP, lead with **15 October** and repeat the same contact paths as above.
