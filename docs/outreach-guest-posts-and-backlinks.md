# Guest posts, backlinks & citations — CTS / ctstours.co.nz

**Site:** `https://www.ctstours.co.nz`  
**Use case:** NZ-based outbound China tours (luxury / small group / tailor-made).  
**Principle:** Few high-trust, relevant links beat hundreds of spammy directory submissions.

---

## 0. Why some links “don’t open” or feel low quality

**Not always the list — often the environment or the target site:**

| Cause | What you see | What to do |
|--------|----------------|------------|
| **Network / region** | Timeouts, “site can’t be reached”, intermittent blank pages | If you are on **restrictive networks** (e.g. mainland China), many overseas SMB sites and PR tools are **slow or blocked**; try **NZ VPN / mobile hotspot**, or ask someone in NZ to confirm the URL once. |
| **Bad hosting / origin down** | Cloudflare **522** / **525** | The domain exists but the **origin server is offline** — nothing you can fix; **skip that target** until it loads reliably in a normal browser. |
| **Wrong hostname** | 404 on `www.` but OK without (or the reverse) | Prefer the URL that returns **200 in a browser**; update bookmarks accordingly (e.g. TAANZ below). |
| **Anti-bot / HEAD blocked** | Tools time out but the site opens in Chrome | **Open in a real browser**; some sites block automated fetchers or disallow `HEAD`. |
| **“Opens but bad for brand SEO”** | Guest-post page loads; homepage is **generic multi-topic / obvious spun articles** | Treat as **low priority** or skip — you want **editorial fit + human audience**, not a link farm. |

**How we quality-check outbound targets:** open in browser → check “About” + recent posts → read guest rules → one short test email. **Do not bulk-submit** to every “write for us” hit from Google.

## 3. Guest post & contributor channels (NZ / travel)

**Status key:** ✅ manually verified page body loads in a fetch tool · ⚠️ verify in your browser before investing time · ❌ do not prioritise for brand SEO.

Verify each site’s **current** “Write for us” / editorial policy before sending. Policies and fees change.

| Channel | URL | Notes |
|---------|-----|--------|
| NZ Travel Tips (guest / sponsored) | https://nztraveltips.com/guest-posting/ | ✅ NZ travel focus; clear guest vs sponsored rules; contact via their **Contact** page from that article. |
| Backpackers Travel Magazine — Write for us | https://www.backpackerstravelmagazine.com/write-for-us/ | ✅ Loads; **backpacker** audience — only pitch if you adapt tone (first China trip, budgeting, hostels/rail). Email on page; some older internal links use `http://`. |
| Planit NZ — Write for us | https://www.planitnz.com/home/write-for-us | ⚠️ Has shown **Cloudflare 522** (origin unreachable) from NZ edge — **open yourself**; if it still fails, skip until stable. |
| NZ Blogs — Write for us | https://nzblogs.com/write-for-us | ❌ Page loads but **recent posts look like generic SEO filler** (unrelated city pairs, etc.). **Not recommended** for CTS brand or “high trust” backlinks. |
| **Research workflow** | Google: `"write for us" travel New Zealand`, `"guest post" China travel`, `"contribute" travel blog Australia` | Prefer **niche fit** (NZ + outbound / Asia / family / silver travel) over raw “write for us” volume. |

**Also consider (manual research, no single URL):**

- **Regional NZ news / lifestyle** sites that run **opinion or travel** columns (Stuff, NZ Herald travel — usually staff or commissioned; pitch editors with a strong NZ hook).
- **Podcasts** (travel / expat / Asian NZ stories): offer **guest episode** + show notes link to one hub page.
- **Alumni / chamber / business associations** newsletters: “Doing business or leisure in China post‑[year]” — one deep piece + link to visa or hub page.

### B. Industry / trust (NZ travel trade)

| Resource | URL | How to use |
|----------|-----|------------|

### C. Local entity & reviews (citations + discovery)

| Resource | URL | How to use |
|----------|-----|------------|
| Google Business Profile | https://business.google.com/ | ✅ Usually most reliable for **local discovery**; keep NAP identical to `/contact`. |
| Bing Places for Business | https://www.bingplaces.com/ | Optional second citation surface. |
| Meta Business Suite (Facebook Page) | https://business.facebook.com/ | Social proof + consistent NAP; not a “SEO directory” but helps **entity consistency**. |
| Pure NZ travel trade context | https://www.newzealand.com/int/travel-trade/ | Context on NZ specialist ecosystem — not a substitute for your own GBP. |

### D. “Partner” and resource links (you control relevance)

- **Hotels / cruise / airline GSA** you work with — ask for **partners** or **guest post** on their blog if they publish traveller content.
- **China DMC / hotel brands** — co‑marketing case studies (“How we host NZ groups in Xi’an”) with a single link to the matching **destination hub** or **tour** URL.

### E. Avoid

- Paid “500 directory” packs, PBNs, irrelevant foreign-language SEO blogs.
- Exact‑match anchor spam (e.g. every link “cheap china tours nz”).
